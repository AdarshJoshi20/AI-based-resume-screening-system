About ResumeRadar

ResumeRadar is a Major Project developed by final year students of Graphic Era University, Dehradun. This platform has been collaboratively built as part of the B.Tech CSE Batch of 2025.

The contributors to this project are:

  • Adarsh Joshi
  • Vasu Kush
  • Apoorva Rana
  • Vipul Bora

ResumeRadar is an AI-powered platform designed to help you find the most suitable resumes for any job description. With a user-friendly interface, recruiters and hiring managers can upload job descriptions and resumes to receive instant matches, improving both efficiency and decision-making.
